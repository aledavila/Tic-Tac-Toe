Tic-Tac-Toe

Player 1: X

Player 2: O

1. Decide who will be player 1 and who will be player 2.

2. When you are ready to start, player 1 will go first.

3. Player 1 will choose any empty space to put an X in.

4. After player 1 is done player 2 will then choose a place to put O in.

5. Repeat this until someone wins or you run out of spaces to click.

6. Don't worry we will alert you to show you who won!

Happy gaming!
